Game Development Workshops

Introducing students to coding and creativity through accessible game design

Program Overview

Our game development workshops introduce students to the fundamentals of coding, design, and digital creativity using the accessible Microstudio platform. Designed for students with no prior experience, these workshops create a supportive environment where participants can build confidence in their technical abilities while creating something fun and engaging.

Workshops are available as single-day introductions or multi-session programs, allowing us to adapt to different educational settings and student needs. Our focus is on making computing concepts accessible and engaging through the universal appeal of game creation.

Workshop Formats

We offer flexible workshop formats to meet the needs of different communities and educational environments. All workshops are led by experienced instructors with backgrounds in both education and game development.

Single-Day Workshop

A 4-hour introduction to game development where students create a simple, complete game they can share with friends and family. Perfect for community centers, libraries, and first-time events.

Multi-Session Series

A 4-6 week program with weekly sessions that build on previous learning. Students develop more complex games and deeper programming skills. Ideal for after-school programs and summer camps.

Impact and Outcomes

Our workshops are designed to do more than teach coding – they help students develop confidence, creativity, and problem-solving skills that transfer to other academic areas. Students leave with a completed game they've built themselves, as well as access to continue developing their skills with our online resources.

— Jasmine, 7th Grade Workshop Participant
