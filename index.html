Our Mission

Good Vibes Computing is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit founded with a vision: to democratize access to advanced computing resources for all, regardless of socioeconomic background or geographic location.

We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to explore the transformative world of computing, robotics, game development, and artificial intelligence - technologies that will define their future.

Through donated and low-cost GPU resources, workshops, and mentorship programs, we're building bridges to technology careers for underrepresented people.

{/* Programs */}

Our Programs

Open Source Game Development

Introducing students to game creation through our accessible open source platforms. Students learn coding, art, and design principles while building their own playable games.

Computing Access

Providing schools and community centers with donated hardware and cloud computing resources to run AI models, data visualization tools, and simulations.

Training

Empowering others with the knowledge and resources to effectively integrate computing, AI and game development into their organizations.

Hackathons

Organizing competitive events where students collaborate to solve real-world problems using open and accessible computing resources.
