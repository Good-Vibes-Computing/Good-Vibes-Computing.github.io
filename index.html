Our Mission

Good Vibes Computing is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit founded with one clear vision: to democratize access to advanced computing resources for all students, regardless of socioeconomic background or geographic location.

We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to explore the transformative world of GPU computing, game development, and artificial intelligence - technologies that will define their future.

Through donated and low-cost GPU resources, workshops, and community events, we're building bridges to technology careers for underrepresented youth.

Our Programs

I. Workshops

Introducing kids of all ages to open source game creation through the accessible Microstudio platform. Students learn coding, art, and design principles while building their own playable games.

II. Computing Access

Providing schools and community centers with donated hardware and cloud resources to run AI models.

III. Training

Empowering community with the knowledge and resources to effectively integrate artificial intelligence.

IV. Hackathons

Organizing competitive events where students collaborate to solve real-world problems using computing resources and game design principles.
